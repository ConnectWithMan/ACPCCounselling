ACPC Third Round of Allotment List 2026

Check Your Seat Allotment for B.Tech & Pharmacy

Access the official ACPC third round of allotment list. Find out the reporting process, token fee payment details, and what to do next.

Schedule for Third Round of Allotment

Sr.No. ACPC Admission Event Dates (2026)
1 Online Consent, Choice Filling & Alteration for Round 3 July 20 – July 23, 2026
2 Declaration of ACPC Third Round of Allotment List July 25, 2026
3 Online Deposition of Token Tuition Fee July 25 – July 28, 2026

How to Check the ACPC Third Round of Allotment List

Once the choice filling concludes, ACPC processes the seat distribution based on the merit list and candidate preferences. Here is how you can check the ACPC third round of allotment list:

  • Step 1: Visit the official ACPC online admission portal at gujacpc.admissions.nic.in.
  • Step 2: Click on the respective link for "B.Tech/Degree Engineering" or "Pharmacy" Admission 2026.
  • Step 3: Log in with your registered User ID, Password, and Security PIN.
  • Step 4: Navigate to the "Seat Allotment Result" section on your dashboard.
  • Step 5: The system will display the college and branch allotted to you. Save or take a printout of the allotment details.

What to Do After Checking the Allotment List?

Finding your name in the ACPC third round of allotment list is a significant step. Here are the actions you can take depending on your satisfaction with the allotted seat:

1. Accepting the Allotted Seat (Confirming Admission)

If you are satisfied with the college and branch allotted in the third round, you must confirm your admission. To do this, you need to pay the required token tuition fee online through the admission portal. Once the payment is successful, download the Admission Letter.

2. Participating in Subsequent Rounds (Final Online / Vacant Seat Round)

If you wish to upgrade your seat, or did not get an allotment in Round 3, you can participate in the subsequent Government/GIA final online round (tentatively scheduled to start from August 1, 2026) or local college-level vacant seat admission rounds for self-financed institutes (SFIs). Candidates must monitor the official vacant seat report published by ACPC after the completion of the third round.

Token Tuition Fee Payment

Candidates listed in the ACPC third round of allotment list must pay a token tuition fee online between July 25 and July 28, 2026 to confirm their seats. The fee amount varies depending on the institute type (Government vs. SFI) and category (General/SC/ST/SEBC). The payment must be made strictly through the online portal (Net Banking, Credit Card, or Debit Card).

Failure to pay the token tuition fee before the deadline will result in automatic cancellation of the allotted seat.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How to check the ACPC third round of allotment list?

You can check the ACPC third round of allotment list by logging into the official ACPC admission portal (gujacpc.admissions.nic.in) using your User ID and password. Go to the 'Seat Allotment Result' section.

What should I do after my name appears in the ACPC third round of allotment list?

If you are satisfied with the allotted seat, confirm your admission by paying the token tuition fee online and downloading the admission letter by July 28, 2026. If you want to participate in subsequent rounds or vacant seat rounds, you can follow the official notifications from ACPC.

Is the token fee refundable if I cancel my admission after Round 3?

Yes, online cancellation of admission for Round 3 is scheduled from July 25 to July 27, 2026. The token fee is refunded after any applicable deductions as per ACPC guidelines.

Can I participate in subsequent rounds if I get a seat in the third round of allotment?

Yes, candidates who wish to upgrade can participate in the subsequent Government/GIA final online round or vacant seat admissions in self-financed institutes (SFI) by following the procedure defined by ACPC.
