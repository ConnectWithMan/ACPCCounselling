ACPDC Counselling 2026 Overview

The Education Department of the Government of Gujarat has constituted the Admission Committee for Professional Diploma Courses (ACPDC) to manage admissions for 3-year Diploma Engineering courses.

Committee Name Admission Committee for Professional Diploma Courses (ACPDC)
Offered Courses Diploma in Engineering & Technology (First Year & D2D)
Admission Base Strictly merit list calculated from 10th Standard (SSC) Marks
Compulsory Subjects Mathematics, Science, and English in Class 10
Registration Mode Online through gujdiploma.admissions.nic.in
Registration Fee ₹250 (Non-Refundable, Paid Online)
Helpdesk Support acpdc.gujarat.gov.in / Helpline: 079-26566000

Step-by-Step Admission Process

A walkthrough of the centralized online admission committee process for candidates.

01

Online Registration

Candidates log on to gujdiploma.admissions.nic.in, sign up as a new candidate, and fill personal details.

02

Document Upload & Fee

Upload marksheet copies, school leaving certificate, category documents, and pay the ₹250 fee online.

03

Merit Rank Allocation

The committee verifies uploaded certificates and generates a provisional and final merit list rank based on SSC marks.

04

Mock Choice Filling

Participate in the practice round of choice filling to test college allotments. Learn how priorities affect options.

05

Round 1 Locking

Fill, prioritize, and lock the final list of colleges. Locked choices are used for actual seat allocation.

06

Seat Confirmation

Download the seat allotment letter, pay the online token fee, and secure your admission at the college.

Eligibility Criteria

Verify details before checking admission status.

  • Qualifying Exam: Candidate must have passed SSC (Class 10th) or equivalent standard.
  • Compulsory Subjects: The qualifying exam must include Mathematics, Science, and English.
  • School Location: The school from which the candidate passed SSC must be located in Gujarat. Non-Gujarat school candidates must satisfy domicile requirements.
  • Selection Metric: Ranks are derived from qualifying 10th marks. No entrance exam is required.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick solutions to common candidate inquiries about ACPDC.

The registration fee is ₹250. This is non-refundable and must be paid online via Net Banking, Credit Card, UPI, or Debit Card during the registration process.

The mock round is a practice trial. It allows you to check which college you might receive based on your merit position and filled choices. The mock round seat allotment is NOT final, and you must lock choices again during Actual Round 1.

The merit rank is calculated based on marks secured in primary subjects (Mathematics, Science, English) in the 10th (SSC) Board exam. General guidelines calculate ranks strictly out of board marks without entrance exams.

TFWS provides full tuition fee exemption in polytechnics. Candidates with family incomes below ₹8 Lakhs per annum who submit a valid income certificate from the competent authority of Gujarat are eligible for TFWS merit allotment.

Candidates with grace marks are eligible to apply as long as they have officially passed the qualifying exam. The marksheet scores (inclusive or exclusive of grace depending on board guidelines) will be used to process merit scores.
