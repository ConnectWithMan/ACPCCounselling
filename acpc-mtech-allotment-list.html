How to Check the ACPC M.Tech Seat Allotment List

Once the choice filling concludes, ACPC processes the seat allotment based on GATE scorecard priorities and Gujarat PGCET merit ranks. Here is how you can check the ACPC M.Tech seat allotment list:

  • Step 1: Visit the official ACPC online admission portal at gujacpc.admissions.nic.in.
  • Step 2: Click on the respective link for "M.E./M.Tech/M.Pharm" Admission 2026.
  • Step 3: Log in with your registered User ID, Password, and Security PIN.
  • Step 4: Navigate to the "Seat Allotment Result" section on your dashboard.
  • Step 5: The system will display the college and branch allotted to you. Save or take a printout of the allotment details.

What to Do After Checking the Allotment List?

Finding your name in the ACPC M.Tech seat allotment list is a significant step. Here are the actions you can take depending on your satisfaction with the allotted seat:

1. Priority for GATE/GPAT Qualified Candidates

GATE-qualified candidates receive direct first priority in seat allotment. Once their seats are confirmed, the remaining vacant seats are allocated to non-GATE candidates based on Gujarat PGCET performance. You can check previous year cutoffs in the ACPC Cutoff guide.

2. Confirming Allotment and Admission

If you receive a seat allotment, you must pay the token tuition fee online via the e-portal to generate your official Admission Letter and report at your allotted institute. You can check different engineering colleges in the ACPC Colleges List.

Token Tuition Fee Payment

Candidates listed in the ACPC M.Tech seat allotment list must pay the token tuition fee online within the specified deadline to confirm their admission. The fee amount varies depending on the institute type (Government vs. SFI) and candidate category as per the ACPC Reservation Policy. The payment must be made strictly through the online portal (Net Banking, Credit Card, or Debit Card).

Failure to pay the token tuition fee before the deadline will result in automatic cancellation of the allotted seat.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How to check the ACPC M.Tech seat allotment list?

You can check the ACPC M.Tech allotment list by logging into the official ACPC portal (gujacpc.admissions.nic.in) using your registered login credentials under the M.E./M.Tech section.

What is the priority order for ACPC M.Tech seat allotment?

First priority is given to GATE-qualified candidates. Once all GATE-qualified candidates are accommodated, remaining vacant seats are allotted to non-GATE candidates based on their performance in the Gujarat PGCET exam.

How does the rescheduling of Gujarat PGCET 2026 affect the allotment dates?

Due to the postponement of the Gujarat PGCET 2026 exam, the seat allotment timetable is revised. The exam and subsequent allotments are tentatively scheduled for August 2026. Keep monitoring the official site for final dates.

Is the token fee refundable if I cancel my M.Tech admission later?

Yes, if you cancel your admission online within the designated window announced by ACPC, the token fee is refunded after deductions as per the prevailing admission rules.
