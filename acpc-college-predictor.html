How the ACPC Predictor Works

The ACPC College Predictor is an advanced tool designed to help students estimate their admission chances in various engineering and pharmacy colleges across Gujarat. The tool uses robust historical data from the official ACPC previous year cutoffs.

By comparing your expected or actual merit rank against the closing ranks of previous counselling rounds, the algorithm intelligently filters out the list of colleges and specific branches where you are highly likely to get admission.

Features of the College Predictor

Our tool aims to provide accurate guidance to students confused during the choice-filling stage. Here is why you should use it:

  • Personalized Results: Filters the data exclusively based on your category (Open, SC, ST, SEBC, EWS) and merit rank.
  • Comprehensive Database: Includes all Government, Grant-in-Aid, and Private (Self-Financed) colleges participating under ACPC.
  • Branch-Specific Insights: Allows you to check admission possibilities for popular branches like Computer Engineering, IT, AI & Data Science, Mechanical, and more.
  • Choice Filling Strategy: Helps you strategize your preference list for the ACPC choice filling process by separating "safe" and "ambitious" colleges.

Steps to Use the Predictor

Predicting your college takes less than a minute. Follow these simple steps:

  • Step 1: Calculate/Check Rank: Wait for the official ACPC provisional merit list to find your exact merit rank, or estimate it based on your GUJCET/Board marks.
  • Step 2: Enter Details: Input your rank, choose your course (B.Tech / Pharmacy), and select your specific reservation category in the form above.
  • Step 3: Hit Predict: Click on the 'Predict My Colleges' button.
  • Step 4: Analyze List: Review the generated list of colleges. We recommend adding the "highly likely" colleges to your safe choices during the actual ACPC counselling.

Note: The predictions are based on historical trends and should be used for reference purposes only. Final seat allotment is determined entirely by the ACPC authorities based on real-time competition and seat matrix changes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the ACPC College Predictor accurate?

The predictor is highly accurate as an indicative tool because it leverages the official opening and closing ranks from the last few years. However, slight variations in final cutoffs may occur due to changes in paper difficulty and student participation volume in the current year.

Can I check chances for TFWS category?

Yes, TFWS (Tuition Fee Waiver Scheme) cutoffs are usually distinct and significantly higher than standard open cutoffs. We recommend treating TFWS seats as separate high-competition entries when planning your choice filling.

What should I do if my predicted chances are low for my dream college?

If your predicted chances are low for your dream college, it is advisable to put it at the very top of your choice list anyway (as an ambitious choice). Always follow it up with a solid mix of moderate and "safe" colleges where your rank comfortably clears the historical cutoff.
